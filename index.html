This Historic motel has a 50's charm with updated amenities. We have clean, comfortable rooms at affordable rates.

Located in downtown Burnside, the only city on Lake Cumberland, let 7 Gables motel be your quiet getaway. We offer fresh coffee and rolls every morning and also have crystal clear swimming pool and corn hole boards for use at your leisure.

We can accommodate parking for guests with boats or RV's and also have parking directly in front of your room for your Street Rod or muscle car. We are a family owned and operated motel for over 50 years and look forward to serving you for many more!
